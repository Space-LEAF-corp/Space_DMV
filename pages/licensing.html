Ceremonial Captain Licenses

This page lists the non-governmental, fictional licenses used in the Space DMV universe to teach responsibility and safety.

Level I – Local Orbit Captain

For captains operating training simulators and local-orbit starship demos. Focused on basic navigation and passenger care.

Level II – Interplanetary Steward

For captains responsible for multi-world routes, ceremonial cargo, and long-duration civilian journeys.

Level III – Ark Guardian

Highest ceremonial rank, entrusted with ark-class vessels and microscopic children missions like Atlas.
