Orientation Briefing

This sequence introduces civilian captains to the ceremonial rules of space travel, remote child safety, and cooperative energy stewardship.

Module 1 – Stewardship

Learn why every space car is registered under a community safety charter, and how captains uphold dignity for kids and passengers.

Module 2 – Remote Engagement

Understand how children participate remotely through ICU glasses and digital tours—never placed at risk in live environments.

Module 3 – Captain’s Seals

Review the ceremonial seals that mark your commitment: Remote Stewardship, Orbital Stewardship, and Planet Worth Living On.
