# Space VIN Specification 1.0  
*Technical and ceremonial identifier for vessels registered under the Space DMV.*

---

## Purpose
The Space VIN (SVIN) is a unique identifier assigned to any vessel capable of leaving Earth’s atmosphere.  
It ensures:

- Clear lineage  
- Safety accountability  
- Operator responsibility  
- Interstellar traceability  
- Non‑weaponization verification
